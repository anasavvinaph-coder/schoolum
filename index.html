Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed for every stage of development.

Preschool

Age 3-5. Play-based learning that fosters curiosity, social skills, and early literacy in a nurturing environment.

Primary School

Grades 1-5. Building strong foundations in Mathematics, Science, and Languages with a focus on inquiry.

Middle School

Grades 6-9. Developing critical thinking, project-based learning, and personal responsibility.

High School

Grades 10-12. Rigorous university preparation, advanced sciences, and leadership opportunities.
